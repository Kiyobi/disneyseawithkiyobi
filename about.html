  • Tokyo DisneySea opening hours

    Normally, the park will be open to welcome visitors from 9 am to 10 pm every day. However, on certain dates and special occasions, there may be changes. To be sure, you should check first at the park's official website.

    With Disneysea popularity, many people still line up to buy tickets hours before opening time. Therefore, make time to get to the park as soon as possible.

  • The "golden" time to go to Tokyo DisneySea

    Taking into account holidays, weather, and other factors, the best time to visit Tokyo DisneySea is before or after Golden Week (early April or late May). During this time, summer hasn't started yet, so you can have fun at the park.

    The main Japanese holidays are also the times when the park is most crowded, such as New Year (late December to early January) and Golden Week (late April to early May) and school holidays learn.

  • Eat and Drink at Tokyo Disneysea

    Tokyo Disneysea is one of the very few Disney parks that sells alcohol. Not just a few beers, but fun cocktails at the kiosks and fine wines at the elegant Teddy Roosevelt Lounge.

    In terms of food, there are also many light and delicious snacks such as caramel & banana sweet fried burrito, Little Green Alien mochi, Chandu Tiger Tail dumplings, classic Disney turkey legs and more.

    Besides, at DisneySea, you can find popcorn with many different flavors like curry, honey, soy sauce and butter, cappuccino and many more. If you're lucky, you'll also get to try limited seasonal scents.
